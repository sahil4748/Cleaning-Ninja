# Brand Identity Rules

## Brand Name
Cleaning Ninja

## Tagline
"Australia's Premium Cleaning Service"

## Brand Personality
- Professional but approachable
- Premium but not pretentious
- Efficient (ninja-like) but thorough
- Eco-conscious and trustworthy

## Logo Usage
- Primary logo: Ninja silhouette + wordmark
- Icon only: Ninja silhouette (for favicon, small spaces)
- Minimum clear space: 2x icon height on all sides
- Never distort, rotate, or add effects to logo

## Voice & Tone
- Confident and reassuring
- Australian English ("Aussies", "fair dinkum", "no dramas")
- Avoid: "cheap", "budget", "quick-fix" - use "value", "premium", "efficient"
- Always include local trust signals (Australian-owned, police checked, insured)

## Imagery Style
- Warm, bright, natural lighting
- Clean, modern Australian interiors
- Diverse Australian families/professionals
- Before/after shots where relevant
- All images must have subtle warm overlay (beige tint, 10-15% opacity)
- No medical masks, hazardous materials, or "industrial" cleaning imagery
